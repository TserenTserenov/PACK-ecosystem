# ECO.SOTA.002: Freemium модели в EdTech (2025-2026)

Freemium остаётся доминирующей моделью в EdTech (92% teachers discover через free trials), но профитабельность достигается редко.

## Success Case: Duolingo (2025)

**Масштаб:**
- 500M+ total users
- 42M MAU
- 10M+ paid subscribers (Q1 2025)
- Conversion free→paid: ~2%

**Revenue Structure:**
- 76% subscriptions ($6.99/mo Duolingo Plus)
- 7% advertising (в free tier)
- 5%+ in-app purchases
- Profitable с Q2 2023

**Почему работает:**
1. Massive addressable market (2B language learners)
2. Free tier = growth engine (97% на free, word-of-mouth)
3. Gamification → habit formation (streaks, leaderboards)
4. Sustainable free tier (ads + IAP покрывают cost of free users)

## Failure Case: Coursera (2025)

**Масштаб:**
- Revenue $757M (2025)
- Net loss: $116.6M (2023) — not profitable

**Почему не работает:**
1. Marketing cost 36.5% of revenue (слабый organic growth)
2. Enterprise NRR 89% (B2B клиенты churn)
3. Free tier не monetized (auditing free без ads = pure cost center)

## Сравнение

| Метрика | Duolingo | Coursera |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Free tier monetization | Ads + IAP | Нет |
| Marketing cost | Low (organic) | 36.5% revenue |
| Profitability | Profitable (2023+) | Net loss $116.6M |
| Word-of-mouth | Strong (42M MAU) | Weak |

## Импликации для IWE

- Free tier должен быть valuable + monetized (ads или basic IAP)
- Word-of-mouth > paid marketing (design for virality)
- B2B модель требует high NRR (>110%), иначе убыточна

## Failure Mode: Freemium Without Free Tier Monetization

- Если free tier = pure funnel (не monetized) → нужен высокий conversion (>5%)
- Если conversion низкий (<3%) → free users = cost, не рост
- Решение: ads, lite IAP, или freemium-trial hybrid (14-day trial → downgrade to limited free)
